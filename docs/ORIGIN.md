# Origin

Portfolio Allocation Risk Studio exists after the scorecards, trust-room, procurement-answer, RFP assembly, proof-gap, board-memo, benchmark, and scenario layers.

The earlier tools show evidence, narrative, and benchmark posture. This repo turns that stack into one allocation-risk surface so leadership can explain what to protect, what to trim, where downside is clustering, and what capital story survives a board or diligence room.
